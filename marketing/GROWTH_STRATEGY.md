## Anti-Patterns (Don't Do These)

- ❌ Buying followers (kills engagement rate, easy to detect)
- ❌ "Follow for follow" chains (attracts wrong audience)
- ❌ Posting without engaging (you're not a broadcast channel)
- ❌ Over-promising benchmarks you can't back up
- ❌ Attacking other projects (contrast is fine, toxicity kills)
- ❌ Spam-tagging investors or influencers
- ❌ Going silent for days (consistency > intensity)

## Core Principles

1. **Value-first every post** — Would you retweet your own post? If not, don't post it.
2. **Show, don't claim** — Demos > descriptions. Data > promises.
3. **Be a human** — Share failures. Be honest about what doesn't work.
4. **Engage like you mean it** — 1 thoughtful reply > 10 generic ones.
5. **Consistency compounds** — 3 posts/week for 12 weeks beats 12 posts in 1 week.
