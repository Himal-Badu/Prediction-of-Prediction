# PoP Content Strategy

## Brand Voice

Himal Badu — 16-year-old AI founder building PoP (Prediction of Prediction). Not corporate. Not hype-y. Real builder energy. Ambitious, authentic, technical. Show the work, not just the results. Humble but confident.

**Sound bite:** *"Every other company watches the LLM's outputs. We watch the LLM's brain."*

---

## Target Audience (in priority order)

1. **AI Researchers & ML Engineers** — The core technical audience. They care about architecture, benchmarks, and novelty. They'll amplify if the work is legit.
2. **Founders & Indie Hackers** — Especially young founders. They connect with the building journey. #BuildInPublic crowd.
3. **Investors (Angel/Seed/VC)** — Looking for signal. Technical credibility + founder story = magnet.
4. **AI Enthusiasts & Early Adopters** — Follow the conversation. Shape public opinion. Word of mouth.

---

## Content Pillars

### 1. Building Journey (30%)
Raw, real-time documentation of building PoP. What's working, what's not, what happened today. The messy reality of building something hard.

- Daily progress updates
- Behind-the-scenes decisions
- Challenges and breakthroughs
- Milestone celebrations

### 2. Technical Insights (25%)
Deep but accessible. Show you know your stuff without gatekeeping. Explain *why* PoP works, not just *that* it works.

- How meta-learning works
- Error detection architecture
- Probability distribution analysis
- Comparisons to existing approaches

### 3. Industry Commentary (25%)
Thought leadership on AI reliability, hallucination, LLM limitations. Hot takes backed by evidence. Positions PoP as solving a real, urgent problem.

- Commentary on AI news
- Hot takes on LLM reliability
- The hallucination problem
- Where AI is heading

### 4. Founder Story (20%)
The human element. 16 years old. No funding. No team. Just code and conviction. This is what makes people root for you.

- Age + ambition narrative
- Learning in public
- Life as a teen founder
- Motivation and vision

## Posting Schedule

**Frequency:** 2-3 posts per day

| Time (UTC) | Content Type | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| 09:00 | Technical insight OR industry comment | Morning scroll. US East Coast waking up, Europe active. |
| 15:00 | Building journey / update | Afternoon. Show what you've been working on today. |
| 21:00 | Hot take OR founder story | Evening. More reflective, personal, or opinionated. |

**Flexibility:** Don't force all 3 slots. Some days 2 is plenty. Quality > volume. Skip a slot rather than post filler.

**Thread days:** 1-2 threads per week (replaces one slot). Threads get higher reach and engagement.

## Hashtag Strategy

### Primary (use 2-3 per post, rotate):
- `#BuildInPublic` — community, visibility, authenticity
- `#AI` — broad reach
- `#MachineLearning` — technical audience
- `#LLM` — directly relevant
- `#AIStartup` — founder/investor audience

### Secondary (use when relevant):
- `#OpenAI` `#Anthropic` `#GPT` — when referencing specific models
- `#NeuralNetworks` — technical deep dives
- `#FounderLife` — personal/journey posts
- `#TeenFounder` — age narrative
- `#AIResearch` — when sharing insights

### Rules:
- Max 3-4 hashtags per post. More looks spammy.
- Place hashtags at the end, not inline (cleaner).
- Thread tweets: hashtags only in the first and last tweet.
- Skip hashtags on pure hot takes — let the take speak.

## Engagement Tactics

### Reply Strategy
- **Reply to every thoughtful comment** in the first 2 hours. Algorithm boost.
- Engage with AI researchers and ML engineers who post about hallucination/reliability.
- Quote-tweet interesting takes with your perspective (adds value, gets visibility).

### Community Building
- Respond to other #BuildInPublic founders. Genuine support, not networking.
- Ask questions in posts — "What's your biggest LLM hallucination story?" drives replies.
- Share other people's good work. Generosity compounds.

### Thread Strategy
- Threads get 2-3x the engagement of single posts.
- Structure: Hook → Problem → Insight → Solution → CTA.
- End threads with a question or invitation to discuss.
- Pin your best-performing thread to profile.

### Timing & Cadence
- Best days: Tuesday-Thursday (highest engagement for tech content).
- Weekends: lighter, more personal content.
- Never post more than 3 times in 24 hours. Respect the feed.

### Growth Hacks
- Quote-tweet AI news with a PoP-relevant angle within 1 hour of breaking news.
- DM people who engage thoughtfully — build real relationships.
- Create a "Day X of building PoP" series — people follow serial content.
- Use polls: "Would you trust an AI that knows when it's wrong?" — high engagement.

---

## Metrics to Track

- **Engagement rate** (replies + retweets + likes / impressions) — target 3-5%
- **Follower growth** — target 100+ new followers/week initially
- **Thread completion rate** — are people reading to the end?
- **DM quality** — are investors/researchers reaching out?
- **Quote tweets** — are people sharing your takes?
